About us


Hello! Welcome on our site. Here we offer the good service for those restaurants who accepts online orders from customers using food ordering services like Zomato, Swiggy etc..

Here you can ease up the work of managing your incoming orders from different apps in one place. All the incoming orders are displayed on home page in one tab, you can accept or decline the orders as you wish. The accepted orders are displayed in other tab.

You can also manage the inventory for your restaurant. And as per the goods in inventory you can estimate how many orders you can take. And you can also view your past orders and other statistics data.
